#3 - Expand Data Stories
What & Why
Currently only 3 stories exist (Digital Leap, Health Transformation, Environmental Crossroads). With 100 datasets available, there's material for many more compelling narratives. Adding 6 new stories gives users richer guided explorations of Jordan's data across economy, demographics, governance, and more.

Done looks like
Stories page shows 9 total stories (3 existing + 6 new)
Each new story has 3-4 chapters with narrative text, animated chart, and highlight badge
Stories are organized with visual variety (different color themes)
All chart data loads correctly from OWID sources
Stories work in both light and dark mode
Story cards on the listing page show clear previews
Out of scope
Interactive quiz or assessment features within stories
User-generated stories
Video or audio narration
Tasks
Economic Transformation story — Jordan's GDP journey from agrarian economy to services-led growth, covering GDP per capita, trade, FDI, and remittances (4 chapters).
The Refugee Burden story — How hosting millions of refugees shaped Jordan's demographics, infrastructure, and economy. Uses refugee population, population growth, water scarcity, and unemployment data (3 chapters).
Energy Independence story — Jordan's shift from 97% energy imports to growing renewable capacity, covering primary energy, solar share, electricity generation, and CO2 per capita (4 chapters).
Youth & Education story — Declining fertility, rising education enrollment, but persistent youth unemployment (NEET). Uses fertility rate, school enrollment, youth NEET, and female labor force data (3 chapters).
Water: The Invisible Crisis story — Jordan as the 2nd most water-scarce country on Earth. Covers freshwater withdrawal, population growth, agricultural land, and climate data (3 chapters).
Urbanization & Infrastructure story — How Jordan went from rural to 93% urban, covering urban population share, electricity access, infrastructure spending, and mobile connectivity (3 chapters).
Add story color themes — Add new color variants (cyan, indigo, pink) to the story color system for visual variety.
